Checking each shipment on the website is too slow

A Vietnam - Korea transport company may have hundreds of import shipments in different clearance stages every day. Customers keep asking "where is my cargo now?".

Done manually, staff must open Korea Customs (UNI-PASS), type each B/L number (HAWB) or cargo management number, wait for the page, read the status, and record it — repeating for every shipment. With a warehouse list of hundreds of shipments, this takes hours daily and can't be updated continuously.

⚠️ Looking up each shipment on UNI-PASS is slow and error-prone. Not knowing which shipment is cleared (수입신고수리) and which is released (반출완료) delays your delivery dispatch.

The Customs Tracking tool from Vietnam - Korea Logistics queries Korea Customs (UNI-PASS) directly by B/L number (HAWB) or cargo management number. A single lookup shows the full progress instantly; upload an Excel warehouse list to scan the status for every shipment at once.

Frequently asked questions

How do I track Korea import customs clearance status?
Enter a B/L number (HAWB) or cargo management number; the system queries Korea Customs (UNI-PASS) directly and shows the full progress: manifest filed → unloading report → warehoused → import declared → cleared (수입신고수리) → released (반출완료).
Can I look up many shipments at once?
Yes. Upload an Excel warehouse list (HAWB column) and scan the clearance status for hundreds of shipments at once.
Is the data accurate?
Data comes directly from Korea's national customs system UNI-PASS via the official cargo clearance progress Open API, updated in real time.
Should I search by MBL or HAWB?
Search by HAWB or cargo management number for full status and progress per shipment. A master B/L (MBL) only returns the HAWB list without detailed status.
How much does it cost?
Single lookups are free. For bulk Excel lookups and multiple accounts, contact support@transflash.app for pricing based on your volume.
Is there a Korean postal code tool too?
Yes. The same system also looks up Korean postal codes from addresses and fills them in bulk via Excel.
